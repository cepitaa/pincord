Privacy policy

Created: November 19th, 2024, 22:34 GMT-3

At Pincord, we respect and protect the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protext the data you provide when you use our bot on Discord and interact with our service through Pinterest. If you choose to use our service, then you hereby agree to the collection and use of information provided by Pinterest and Discord as described in this policy.

This may contain personal identifiable information. The information that we collect is not collectable by other users.

What do we exactly collect?

When you interact with the Pincord bot, we collect the following data:

  • Discord information:
    • Such as your discord user ID, username, display name, avatar and display avatar.
  • Pinterest information:
    • Data provided during the OAuth authentication with Pinterest, including your Pinterest user ID, boards, and pins. We only collect data you choose to share with the bot through the authentication process.

How is the information used?

The information collected is used solely to operate the Pincord bot and provide you with its features. Specifically, it allows us to:

  • Authenticate your Pinterest account and link it with your Discord account.
  • Allow the bot to interact with your Pinterest data (such as retrieving pins and boards) as per your granted permissions.
  • Identify and manage your account on the bot's system, including tracking your interactions and commands within Discord.

Changes in this policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.
